Understanding Salary, Wages, Commission

Salary: Fixed amount paid regularly (monthly/yearly). Example: ₱20,000/month.

Wages: Paid based on hours or days worked. Formula: Wages = Hourly Rate × Hours Worked or Daily Wage × Days Worked

Commission: Payment based on sales. Formula: Commission = Sales × Commission Rate

Tips: Always identify if pay is fixed (salary), time-based (wages), or performance-based (commission).

External Definitions

Salary vs Wages (monthly vs hourly/daily): Investopedia – Salary vs. Hourly Pay

Commission: Payment based on sales. (Investopedia)

Formulas

  • Weekly Salary from Daily Wage = Daily Wage × Number of Days Worked in a Week
  • Weekly Salary from Annual Salary = Annual Salary ÷ 52
  • Monthly Salary from Daily Wage = Daily Wage × Number of Working Days in Month
  • Monthly Salary from Annual Salary = Annual Salary ÷ 12
  • Gross Income = Salary + Wages + Commission
  • Net Pay = Gross Income – Taxes/Deductions
